Periodic reflection: synthesize patterns, surface insights, recalibrate priorities.

Run weekly (e.g., Friday afternoon) or whenever you need to step back and see the bigger picture.

## Steps

   - `.lead/context/priorities.md`
   - `.lead/context/principles.md`
   - `.lead/context/team.md`
   - All journal entries from the past 7 days (or since last reflect)
   - All person files in `.lead/people/` (scan for recent entries)
   - Recent decision files in `.lead/decisions/`
   - Check for any previous reflect entries in journal to compare
